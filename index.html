KNM B.Ed COLLEGE KANJIRAMKULAM.

proudly Presents

CRESCENDO 2k19.

Inter college literary, arts & cultural fest 25 March 2019

Explore more

With immense delight and dignity, KNM B.Ed College proudly presents the first ever inter college literary, arts and cultural fest Crescendo 2k19 . This fest has been proposed to be conducted on March 25, 2019, at Drishya auditorium kanjiramkulam. We are too proud to host such a great venture and is our privilege and honor. To be a part of this fest should be an ever memorable event in your life. An inter-college competition has also been incorporated to bring out the creative caliber of students. We request your wholehearted co-operation.
